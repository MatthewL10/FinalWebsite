Skystone(2020)

Drivetrain

Custom drivtain built with water jet cut 1/4" aluminum plates and 4 mecanum wheels, which allows for omnidirectional movement. The 1/4" aluminum creates a high strength, yet lightweight drivetrain because of the pocketing on the plates. The pocketing reduces weight while keeping as much strucutral integreiy as possible.

Intake Mechanism

Compliant wheel intake. Built from 3d printed plates and soft grippy wheels to grab hold of plastic blocks. Each side is powered by one motor, so the intake has the strength of two motors to quickly and efficiently intake the game elements. Rubber bands are also attached to tension the two sides to be able to intake blocks from many different angles.

Outtake mechanism

This is a 3d printed virtual 4 bar linkage with a gripper attached to grab hold of field elements. It is powered by high torque servos, which allows for consistent and fast linkage movement. To assist the motors rubber bands are attached to fight gravuty and reduce the moment of force on the servos. Chains are used to create te virutal 4 bar linkage to constantly keep the gripper horizontal.

This robot uses linear slides to give the robot verticle reach and to stack the game elements. The mecanum wheels allow the robot to quickly move around the field between robots and obstacles. The 3d printed intake allows the robot to quickly intake blocks no matter their orientation and position, which then moves to the gripper that is attached to virtual 4 bar on the verticle linear slides. The virtual 4 bar linkage is then able to quickly extend out to outake the plastic blocks.
