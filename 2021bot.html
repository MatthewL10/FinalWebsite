Ultimate Goal(2021)

Drivetrain

Our boxtube drivetrain allows for efficient interchangeability, mostly due to its sleeker design which combats traditional c-channels. In order to account for the 18”x18” constraint. The custom boxtube drivetrain creates more space for the intake, control and rev hubs, as well as a wobble goal mechanisms.

Intake Mechanism

The intake consists of two stages. The first stage is a motor powered row of compliant rubber wheels that is tensioned downwards to intake discs. After the discs go through the first stage, they enter the polycord which then pulls the discs into the magazine to be ready to be shot into the goal.

Outtake mechanism

Instead of a traditional flicker to flick the discs into the shooter we used a conveyor belt to more quickly fire our shots. The conveyor belt also has zipties to help move the discs into the shooter.

This robot uses it's fast and grippy intake to quickly gather 3 discs into the magazine where they lay ontop of a conveyor belt. The robot is then able to quickly shoot the rings into the top goal. The robot also has a little arm that is able to grab a wobble goal during auto to place onto the designated spots. This robot is also called Robert in memory of our old team captain.
